TOP 5 MINECRAFT MODPACK



1) RLCraft



RLCraft is one of the most well-known modpacks. It’s been played by various famous YouTubers like Dream and PewDiePie. However, it’s most widely known for being one of the most brutal modpacks.

First, survival mode has been tweaked, as you’ll now have to keep yourself hydrated and regulate your body temperature. Additionally, food is no longer your only lifeline as eating won’t heal you. And if that wasn’t hard enough, this modpack comes with formidable enemies, like aggressive dragons from Ice & Fire, new demons, monsters, and so much more.

So if you’re looking for a new challenge, this modpack will push you to your limits



2) SkyFactory 4



SkyFactory 4 is essentially SkyBlock with mods. It’s the same old survival map where players have as few resources as possible, except it’s been supercharged with mods. If you haven’t heard of SkyBlock, you should try it out on one of these Minecraft servers!

In this modpack, players will experience the magic, technology, and quality-of-life mods. These include Astral Sorcery, Carry On, and Building Gadgets. If you’re looking for a new Minecraft experience, this modpack is for you.



3) Pixelmon



Described as Minecraft meets Pokemon, this modpack is for all fans of both games! Pixelmon adds a variety of Pokemon to the game for you, the player, to catch. However, the pack doesn’t end there. It also includes adventure aspects like Biomes O’Plenty, Journey mod, Explorers Compass, and more!

The best part about this modpack is the vast amount of Minecraft servers available! So form teams and play with your friends in a world of Pokemon. Catch, train and breed different Pokemon types and become one of the best trainers among friends!



4) All The Mods



This modpack is one of the best Minecraft packs if you’re looking for customizability. Its focuses on equipment, locomotions, flora, fauna, technology, energy, and automation by combining a plethora of mods. Your Minecraft world will never be the same again!

5) Better Minecraft



Better Minecraft is one of the most popular modpacks with a solid 4.3M downloads from CurseForge. As it suggests, the name is an enhanced version of Vanilla Minecraft with new biomes, dungeons, structures, and mobs. It completely overhauls the game yet keeps the classic Minecraft nostalgia we know and love.

Get ready to go questing, explore new abandoned Mineshafts, teleport through waystones and fight off new Pillager types. From starting a farm with various plants like grapes, tomatoes, and rice, to taming the Ender Dragon – there’s something for everyone!
